Shawarma Recipe

Description

Chicken shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food traditionally made with spiced meat thatâ€™s stacked on a vertical rotisserie and roasted.

Ingredients

  • Skinless and Boneless Chicken Breast or Thighs
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Olive Oil
  • Cooking Oil

Steps:

  1. Slice the chicken into thin strips.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, minced garlic, spices, olive oil, and a little salt to make a marinade.
  3. Add the chicken to the marinade, mix well, and let it sit for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor).
  4. Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the marinated chicken until fully cooked and slightly crispy on the edges.
  6. Serve the chicken in pita bread or flatbread with your favorite toppings and sauces.
