Cheese Cake Recipe

Description

A cheesecake is a dessert characterized by a thick, creamy filling made from soft cheese

often cream cheese, but also ricotta or quark), eggs, and sugar, baked or chilled.

It usually has a crust made from crushed cookies, graham crackers, pastry, or sometimes sponge cake.

Ingredients

  • Butter, for coating the pan.
  • 2 pounds. full-fat cream cheese, at room temperature.
  • 1 cup. granulated sugar.
  • 1 tablespoon. cornstarch, or 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (optional)
  • 1/8 teaspoon. salt.
  • 1/2 cup. sour cream.
  • 2 teaspoons. freshly squeezed lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon. vanilla extract.

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven and prepare the pan by coating it with butter.
  2. Make the crust by mixing crushed cookies with melted butter, then press into the pan and bake briefly.
  3. Beat cream cheese until smooth, then add sugar, cornstarch (or flour), salt, sour cream, lemon juice, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing gently.
  5. Pour the filling over the crust.
  6. Bake until the center is almost set.
  7. Let the cheesecake cool, then refrigerate for several hours or overnight before serving.
